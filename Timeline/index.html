Hideaki Anno

Animator, Director, Screenwriter

Background

Anno started his career after attending Osaka University of Arts and soon became an Animator for a Macross show. He didn't gain notable recognition until he worked on Hiyao Miyazaki's "Nausicaa of the Valley of the Wind." Miyazaki was impressed by his drawings and had him animate the more complicated scenes at the end of the movie.

Anno Co-founded animation studio Gainax in 1984, where they created some very good cartoons, until recently, where everyone left for studio trigger :,^).

In 1988, he made his first directorial debut in the sci fi OVA Diebuster. You can see inspiration for some of his later characters from he characters in this show.
